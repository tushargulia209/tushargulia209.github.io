About MET City

Reliance MET City is a massive, 8,250-acre integrated industrial township in Jhajjar, Haryana, designed as a world-class ecosystem for global industries and modern communities. Strategically located near the Delhi-NCR region, it offers exceptional connectivity via the KMP Expressway and major road networks linking it to Gurgaon and Bahadurgarh. This ambitious project is rapidly becoming a premier economic hub, attracting significant national and international investment across manufacturing, logistics, and automotive sectors.
